# Settings

**Settings** is where you prepare the small labels and shortcuts your whole team reuses every day.

Think of it as organizing your filing cabinet before busy chat hours: once labels, lists, stages, and snippets exist, everyone can use them the same way in Inbox, Contacts, Tickets, and Broadcasts.

---

## What you can set up

| Area in Settings | Plain-language purpose | Detailed guide |
|------------------|------------------------|----------------|
| **Labels** | Color tags on a customer (VIP, Complaint, …) | [Workspace tags & lists](./workspace-tags) |
| **Lists** | Groups of contacts (Newsletter, Hot leads, …) | [Workspace tags & lists](./workspace-tags) |
| **Lead stage** | Where the customer is in your sales steps | [Workspace tags & lists](./workspace-tags) |
| **Lead source** | How the customer found you | [Workspace tags & lists](./workspace-tags) |
| **Lifecycle stage** | Broader journey stage (also filters Inbox) | [Workspace tags & lists](./workspace-tags) |
| **Ticket type** | Category for support tickets | [Workspace tags & lists](./workspace-tags) |
| **Snippets** | Ready-made replies you insert with a shortcut | [Snippets](./snippets-usage) |
| **Notification unsubscribe** | Public page linked from emails to turn alerts off | [Notification unsubscribe](./notification-unsubscribe) |

---

## Open Settings

1. In the left sidebar, click **Settings** (near the bottom, with Organization).
2. On the left of the Settings page you will see two groups:
   - **Tags** — Labels, Lists, Lead stage, Lead source, Lifecycle stage, Ticket type
   - **Messaging** — Snippets

> **Gợi ý ảnh:** Menu trái **Settings** với nhóm **Tags** và **Messaging → Snippets**.

---

## Who should change Settings?

Usually an **admin** or team lead sets these up once, then fine-tunes when the business process changes.

If a create button is missing, ask your workspace admin for permission.

---

## Suggested order for a new workspace

1. Create **Lifecycle stages** your team already uses (for example New → In progress → Done).
2. Create a few **Labels** and **Lists**.
3. Add **Ticket types** that match common issues (Refund, Shipping, Account).
4. Write 5–10 daily **Snippets** (greeting, store hours, shipping time).
5. Tell the team where to find them in [Inbox](../operations/inbox-usage) and [Contacts](../operations/contact-management).
