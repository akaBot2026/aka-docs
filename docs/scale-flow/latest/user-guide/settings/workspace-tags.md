# Workspace tags and lists

These Settings items are small building blocks for organizing customers and tickets. They look similar on screen, but each one has a different everyday job.

You create them once in **Settings**. Your team then picks them while chatting, editing a contact, creating a ticket, or building a Broadcast audience.

---

## Quick meaning of each type

| Name in Settings | Everyday meaning | Where you will see it later |
|------------------|------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Labels** | Sticky notes on a contact (VIP, Angry, Wholesale) | Contact page, Inbox contact panel, Broadcast conditions |
| **Lists** | Groups of people (Summer campaign, VIP buyers) | Contacts sidebar, Broadcast **By List**, bulk moves |
| **Lead stage** | Sales step (New lead, Negotiation, Won) | Contact create/edit |
| **Lead source** | How they found you (Facebook ad, Walk-in, Referral) | Contact create/edit, Broadcast conditions |
| **Lifecycle stage** | Bigger journey stage (Lead, Customer, Churn risk) | Contact fields, Inbox filters and conversation header |
| **Ticket type** | Kind of support case (Refund, Delivery, Technical) | Create/edit ticket, Analytics |

**Example:** A fashion shop creates Label `VIP`, List `Newsletter`, Lifecycle stage `Customer`, and Ticket type `Exchange`. Staff apply them without inventing new names each time.

---

## Open a tag page

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Under **Tags**, click the type you need (for example **Labels** or **Lifecycle stage**).
3. You will see a searchable list of existing items.

> **Gợi ý ảnh:** Trang **Settings → Labels** (hoặc Lifecycle stage) với ô Search, nút **Add**, và danh sách thẻ màu.

---

## Create a new item

The steps are almost the same for every type:

1. Click **Add** (the button shows the type name, such as **Add Labels**).
2. In the dialog:
   - Enter a clear **Display name** (what people read)
   - Pick a **Color** so the list is easy to scan
3. Click **Create tag**.

ScaleFlow also keeps a short internal name based on your display name. You usually only need to care about the display name and color.

> **Gợi ý ảnh:** Hộp thoại **Create** với **Display name**, chọn **Color**, và nút **Create tag**.

---

## Edit, reorder, or delete

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Edit** | Open the item, change display name or color, click **Update tag** |
| **Reorder** | Drag the handle (if your role allows managing contacts) so important stages appear first |
| **Delete** | Choose delete and confirm **Delete this tag?** |

Be careful when deleting: items already used on contacts or tickets may disappear from filters or look empty until you assign a new value.

---

## Practical tips

1. Keep names short and familiar to your team (`VIP`, not `Very important person level 1 gold`).
2. Agree on **Lifecycle stages** early — they also appear as Inbox filters.
3. Create **Lists** before big [Broadcasts](../operations/broadcast-usage) so audience picking is easy.
4. Match **Ticket types** to how managers want to read [Analytics](../operations/analytics-usage).
5. Do not create dozens of nearly identical labels — people will stop using them.
