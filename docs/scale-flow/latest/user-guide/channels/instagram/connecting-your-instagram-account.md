# Connect Your Instagram Account

**Instagram** lets customers message your business on Instagram. Connecting it brings those chats into ScaleFlow [Inbox](../../operations/inbox-usage), so your team (and AI) can reply in the same place as Zalo or Messenger.

You do not need to write code. ScaleFlow opens a Meta / Instagram sign-in window, you approve access, and the account appears under **Connected accounts**.

---

## Before you start

- Access to the **Instagram professional account** (or Meta Business setup) you want to connect
- Permission to manage that Instagram account / linked Facebook Page where Meta asks for it
- Permission to manage **Channels** in ScaleFlow

Plan about **10–20 minutes** for the first connection.

---

## Step 1 — Open the Instagram channel page

1. Sign in to ScaleFlow.
2. Open **Channels** in the left menu.
3. Select **Instagram**.
4. Review the **Channel setup** area and the **Connected accounts** list.

![Open Insta](/static/img/open-insta.png)

---

## Step 2 — Click Connect

1. Click **Connect**.
2. Wait while ScaleFlow shows status such as **Redirecting…**, **Connecting…**, or **Waiting for authorization…**.
3. If you change your mind, click **Cancel**.

A Meta / Instagram window should open so you can sign in and approve.


---

## Step 3 — Sign in and approve

1. Sign in with the Meta / Facebook account that manages your Instagram business presence.
2. Select the correct Instagram account (and related Page if asked).
3. Review the permissions ScaleFlow requests.
4. Click **Continue** / **Allow** to finish.

When it works, ScaleFlow shows a success message and the account appears in **Connected accounts**.

![Sign in](/static/img/connect-insta-3.png)

---

## Step 4 — Test the connection

1. On the Instagram row in **Connected accounts**, click **Test**.
2. Wait for **Testing…** to finish.
3. Send a real test Direct Message to your business Instagram from another personal account.
4. Open [Inbox](../../operations/inbox-usage) and confirm the message arrives with an Instagram label.

![Test the connection](/static/img/connect-insta-success.png)
---

## Manage connected Instagram accounts

| Action | When to use it |
|--------|----------------|
| **Test** | Check whether ScaleFlow can still reach Instagram |
| **Reconnect** | Sign in again if messages stop arriving |
| Rename (if shown) | Give the connection a clear internal name |
| **Delete** | Remove the connection (new Instagram messages stop coming into Inbox) |

Empty list tip: if you see **No connected accounts here**, click **Connect** to add the first one.

---

## After Instagram is connected

1. Add FAQs to [Knowledge](../../scaleflow-ai/knowledge-usage) so AI can answer common product questions.
2. Turn on [AI Assistant](../../scaleflow-ai/ai-assistant) when you are ready for help inside Inbox.
3. (Optional) On [Chat Widget](../chat-widget/setting-up-chat-widget), you can show Instagram as an extra contact button if Instagram is already connected.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Connect window does not open

- Allow pop-ups for ScaleFlow in your browser, then try **Connect** again.

### Wrong Instagram account connected

- Use **Delete**, then **Connect** again and carefully select the correct account.

### Messages do not appear in Inbox

1. Click **Test**, then **Reconnect** if the test fails.
2. Confirm Instagram messaging is enabled for your professional account on Meta’s side.
3. Send another test DM and refresh Inbox.

### I cannot see Channels or Connect

- Ask your admin to grant channel management permission.
