# About akaBot Hub

## What is the mission of akaBot Hub?

![image-20230222160249-1.png](/static/img/image-20230222160249-1.png)

In the current context where automation is becoming increasingly popular, enterprises typically have a dedicated CoE team responsible for creating automation products for use by employees within the organization. As employees utilize these automation products, they may have ideas for further improvements, which they can then share with the CoE team. The CoE team will then work to implement these ideas and make the automation products more user-friendly and accessible to employees. Over time, some employees may develop their own ability to create and implement automation solutions, which they can then submit to the CoE team for review and governance.

As businesses continue to adopt large-scale automation, the need for effective management of automation ideas and products becomes increasingly important. This is where akaBot Hub comes in, with a mission to **help businesses manage their automation products and ideas throughout the automation integration process.** By providing a centralized location for idea generation, implementation, and governance, akaBot Hub streamlines the automation process and helps businesses maximize the benefits of automation.

## What are the benefits that akaBot Hub can bring to enterprises ? 
akaBot Hub is a product designed for the RPA Centre of Excellence (CoE) in every company to drive process automation by

- Centrally manage hundreds of thousands of automation projects on the same platform
- Optimizing operation time by providing a platform for employees to share their own automation ideas by themselves instead of letting employees wait for BAs to collect information about automation ideas.
- Evaluating the feasibility of automation ideas in seconds right after employees complete filling in process details information
- Centrally managing and storing millions of files like documents, and source codes,… for automation projects
- Easily keep track cost and benefits of all automation projects
